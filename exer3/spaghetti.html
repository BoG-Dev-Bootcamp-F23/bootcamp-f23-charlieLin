Spaghetti w/ Beef

Ingrediants

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Seasonings
  1. Boil around 4 quarts of water. Salt to taste.
  2. Pour pasta into boiling water and cook to box directions.
  3. Drain pasta and set aside.
  4. Brown the ground beef. Season towards end of cooking.
  5. Heat up tomato sauce in the same pan as the ground beef or in a separate pot.
  6. Season tomato sauce if desired.
  7. Mix the pasta, beef, and sauce together, or put the ingrediants in separate containers for portion control.
